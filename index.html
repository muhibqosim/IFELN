Who We Are

Islamic Finance Emerging Leaders Network

Islamic Finance Emerging Leaders Network (IFELN) is a non-profit, non-sectarian, capacity-building initiative established to bridge the gap between interest and participation in Islamic Banking and Finance, particularly among youth, startups and innovators and entrepreneurs. Despite the steady growth of Islamic finance in Nigeria and globally, many young people and businesses remain excluded not by law, but by lack of awareness, skills and access. Islamic Finance Emerging Leaders Network seeks to address this challenge by delivering practical education, opportunity awareness and hands-on guidance that enables participants to understand Islamic finance, recognize its opportunities and confidently apply its principles in real financial and business decisions.

IFELN's expected outcomes & impact includes: increased Islamic finance literacy among youth and entrepreneurs; more startups accessing Shariah-compliant financing; reduced misconceptions around Islamic finance; stronger ethical finance culture and, a growing pipeline of Islamic finance professionals and innovators.

Our Vision, Mission and Core Objectives

Vision

To build an inclusive ecosystem where youth, startups and entrepreneurs actively leverage Islamic finance for ethical, sustainable economic growth.

Mission

To make Islamic Banking and Finance understandable, accessible and usable by equipping participants with the knowledge, skills and connections required to engage the sector confidently.

Objective

To simplify Islamic Banking and Finance for non-specialists.
To educate youth and entrepreneurs on Shariah-compliant financial tools.
To expose startups to Islamic funding and investment structures.
To build capacity for ethical and sustainable business financing.
To connect participants with industry opportunities, institutions and experts.
